Quick Tips for First-Time Visitors

  • Buy tickets online to skip long lines.
  • Learn basic French phrases — locals appreciate the effort!
  • Use public transportation — the Paris Metro is cheap and efficient.
  • Wear comfortable shoes — you’ll walk more than you think.
