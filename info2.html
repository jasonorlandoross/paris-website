Places to Visit

Markets & Shopping

From the bustling Marché d'Aligre to vintage shopping in the Marais, Paris offers unique finds at every turn. Stop by Free'P'Star and Kilo Shop for vintage fashion, or stroll Rue de la Verrerie for hidden gems.

Charming Streets

Walk along Canal Saint-Martin in the 10th Arrondissement for a local Parisian vibe. It's ideal for a slow afternoon, a coffee in hand, and people-watching from the bridges.

Where to Eat

Must-Try Restaurants

  • Brasserie Bellanger – classic French comfort food near Gare du Nord.
  • Bouillon Julien – affordable Art Nouveau dining with timeless Parisian flair.
  • L'As du Fallafel – famous falafel wraps on Rue des Rosiers in the Marais.
  • Pink Mamma – popular Italian spot with Instagram-worthy interiors in Pigalle.
  • Girafe – upscale dining with Eiffel Tower views (reservations recommended).

Tips for Exploring

  • Public transit is easy and safe — the Metro and RER trains connect all major areas.
  • Even in summer, bring layers. Weather can shift quickly, especially at night.
  • Plan for lots of walking. Many of Paris’s best sights are just blocks apart.

Pro Tip

For the best views, visit the Galeries Lafayette rooftop around sunset — it’s free and offers a stunning panorama of the Paris skyline.
