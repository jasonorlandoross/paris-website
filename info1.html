Discover Paris

Where History Meets Elegance

Paris is a city rich with history, dating back to Roman times. Today, it is known not just for its architecture and art, but also for its romance and charm.

Here are a few must-see landmarks when visiting Paris:

  • The Eiffel Tower
  • The Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica
  • Champs-Élysées and the Arc de Triomphe
